Discounted Cash Flow (DCF)

$$ PV = \frac{CF}{(1 + r)^n} $$

Net Present Value (NPV)

$$ NPV = \sum_{t=0}^{N} \frac{CF_t}{(1+r)^t} $$

Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR)

$$ CAGR = \left( \frac{V_f}{V_i} \right)^{\frac{1}{n}} - 1 $$
